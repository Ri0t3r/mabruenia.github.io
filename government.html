The government of Mabruenia is a constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary system.

The Crown serves as the ceremonial head of state, while governance is carried out by elected officials and appointed ministers.

The legislative branch consists of Parliament, including the House of Representatives and the House of Lords.

The executive branch is led by the Chancellor, who is appointed by the Crown and confirmed by the people, and is responsible for implementing national policy.

The judiciary operates independently to interpret the laws of Mabruenia and ensure justice is upheld.

This system balances royal authority with democratic governance while maintaining justice and fairness.
