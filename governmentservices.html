Government Services

The Government of the Gaelic Kingdom of Mabruenia provides or plans to provide a range of services to citizens and residents. These services are intended to support public welfare, safety, and development.

Overview

Government services are functions offered by a nation to its citizens. Typically, these include:

  • Public Education
  • Healthcare
  • Infrastructure Maintenance
  • Public Safety
  • Social Welfare Programs

Currently, these services are in development and not fully operational. The following projects are underway:

  • Mabruenia TeleCom: Our national phone and communication service. In progress.
  • Higher Education: Colleges and other tertiary education programs. In progress.
  • Mabruenian Central Bank: National bank using the Éclat currency. In progress.

Citizen Identification Requirements

All citizens must possess a Royal Digital Identity Number (RDIN) along with a government-issued ID. If a physical ID cannot be obtained, a valid RDIN is mandatory to access government services.
