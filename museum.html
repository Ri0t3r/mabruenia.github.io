Artifacts Present

Uncirculated Coins

The US mint collection, currently stored in Mabruenia's capital. 12 coins total, 6 from Philadelphia, 6 from Denver. Includes the 1979-P and 1979-D Susan B. Anthony dollars, business strike condition.

Irkladian Pipe

The pipe of the first Queen Consort of Mabruenia, Queen Alexandria Irikladis-Fleming I. The pipe is Burnt slightly on the end. And is Alexandra's favorite pipe.

Imperial Wreath of Olivandria/Lillia

There were 3 official regalia, in 2019 it was the king's tie (no long lost), in 2021-2024 it was the black beret and in 2024 it was is the imperial wreath. The wreath was bought in a place in Greece on holiday, it was cheap and it fit best. Although only 3 official portraits had it. The former Czar did not wear it every day as he may claimed too, for opting out instead to face swap his face on famous leaders by using their video game depecitons. The wreath symbolised autocracy in olivandria and later the monarchy in Lillia. Though the crown has no relevance today, it's still a reminder of what came before Lillia as the wreath is associated with the Czar and the Czar is the founder, the founder of olivandria.

Olivandrian mid-late republic, communist state and early imperial Armies: skirmishers uniform

The uniform you see here lasted from 2019 to 2024. The first version was a green jacket, beige trousers and a black beret. That was the common usage of the uniform and mostly used even with the 2nd variention. The secon varient had DPM, although it wasn't used in any conflicts, It was the standard issue uniform of the Olivnadrian Home Guard. As the first issue (the green jacket) was Widley available to the army at the time (which consisted 3-5 before 2024). As only one person was in the Home guard, it didn't see much action. But its still a peice of history. Note that there was a "winter varient" and that was replacing the trousers from beige to black.

The first Constitution of the Olivanria

This was the first Legal code for the olivandrian nation and government. It was simply and basic for a micronation of 12 people at the time. Freedom from slaverty, abolsihment of class, Fomration of an army, free public services, national symbol (a beetle), formation of a flag and signage by the 4 groups (now named paritrs) of the confederation. It was deeply flawed, the republic lasted 9 months before a communist dictatorship rose to power, The leaders of the republic changed many titles. But the position was known as the consulate. The longest of that was Eillies who lasted 3.5 months in the position as she ushered the eillie golden age. The former king (who was the founder) was placed as head of an army which consisted of die hard loyalist to the the republic (which not only gave him some compensation but prevented his rise to power again), the biggest regret however was the formation of the commiestan sister republic, which lead to the commiestan war to which it lead to the republics downfall
