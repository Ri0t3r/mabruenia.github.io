National Symbols of the Kingdom of Mabruenia

National Flag

The national flag represents the unity and sovereignty of the Kingdom.

National Coat of Arms

The coat of arms serves as the official emblem of the Crown and the State.

Royal Coat of Arms

The Royal Coat of Arms serves as the official emblem of the Crown and the House of Fleming.

National Motto

"Let the Deed Shaw"

National Anthem

The national anthem reflects the heritage and aspirations of the Kingdom.

Lyrics available upon official publication.

National Colors

  • Red – The blood of those who have died for our freedom
  • Blue– The blue sky above our lands
  • White – Peace and Unity

National Animal

The national animal is the dog, symbolizing loyalty and companionship.

National Flower

The national flower is the Mexican Golden Poppy, representing the natural beauty of our lands.

National Food

The national food is tacos, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of our region and the diversity of our culture.

Additional Symbols

Ministry of Justice

The Ministry of Justice is responsible for upholding the rule of law and ensuring justice for all citizens. The symbol of the Ministry is a balanced scale, representing fairness and impartiality in the administration of justice.

War Flag

The war flag is used by the armed forces during times of conflict and represents the strength and resilience of our military. It features a the zia sun symbol, with the colors of Scotland, and the Mabruneian flag in the corner.

Grand Assembly

The Grand Assembly is the legislative body of the Kingdom, responsible for making laws and representing the interests of the citizens.

Ministry of War & Defense

The Ministry of War and Defense is responsible for the defense and security of the Kingdom.

Supreme Court of Mabruenia

The Supreme Court is the highest judicial authority in the Kingdom, responsible for interpreting the law and ensuring justice is served.

Further national symbols may be designated by Royal Decree.
