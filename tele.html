Welcome to the IMTU.

The IMTU is a micronational organization that was de-facto established on April 12th, 2026 during a call between the leaders of Lithoria and Aurelia. The current President of IMTU is Dimitri Arabatzis of the United Provinces of Lithoria. The current Vice-President of IMTU is a representative of Commonwealth of Hnaiisi.
