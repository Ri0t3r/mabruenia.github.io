Welcome to the College

The Mabruenian Kingdom Community College (MKCC) is the national institution for higher learning within the Kingdom of Mabruenia. Operated under the Ministry of Education and Culture, the college provides academic, vocational, and civic education opportunities.

Programs Offered

  • Political Science & Government Studies
  • History of Mabruenia and Micronational Studies
  • Digital Management & Web Development
  • Military and Strategic Studies
  • Public Administration

Admissions

Enrollment is open to all registered citizens of Mabruenia. Applicants must possess a valid Royal Digital Identity Number (RDIN).

Apply for Citizenship | Access Citizen Portal

Campus Status

The college is currently in development and operates primarily as a digital institution. Future expansion plans include structured courses, certifications, and academic records.

Official Notice

All programs are subject to approval by the Ministry of Education and Culture. Unauthorized institutions claiming affiliation with MKCC are not recognized.
